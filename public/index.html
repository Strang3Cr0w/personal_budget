Personal Budget - Let's start a budget!


Starting a budget is essential to reaching your financial goals. Without a budget you have no way of keeping track of what you spend and thus no way of keeping yourself inline with your financial goals. Additionally, budgeting doesn't have to be hard. With this easy budgeting software, you can easily start a budget and keep track of what and when you spend. You can also view a summary of your budget on the summary page, just click the link on the right within the nav menu. Let's get started!

Let's start by inputting the max amount you want to spend each month per category. The first two categories are considered fixed expenses, mortgage/rent and utilities. These are expenses that you will have to pay no matter what.

The following categories are variable expenses. You have control over how much you spend on these categories. Set some realistic, attainable maximums for your variable expenses. We will take what you spend and compare it against your income. Ideally, the difference should be positive and you can then put that money towards your financial goals!

Once you've entered the absolute maximum you're willing to spend each month for every category, save it with the submit button. You can always change these amounts later as needed. Then, go to the expenses page to enter your expenses.
